Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Tindrels ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use Osilate (the "App").

Information We Collect

This Usage Data may include information such as your device operating system version, the pages of our app that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages unique device identifiers, and other diagnostic data.

  • User-generated Content: We collect step counts and chat data.

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

  • Request deletion of your data
